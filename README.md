# Islamic Prayer App Support

A sophisticated and intuitive mobile application designed to seamlessly bridge your daily life with your spiritual commitments. By offering a comprehensive dual calendar display, the app ensures you can view Gregorian and Hijri dates side by side with unparalleled clarity and ease. Never miss a moment of devotion with our highly accurate prayer times, which utilize advanced GPS-based calculations to provide precise timings tailored specifically to your exact geographic location. Finding the sacred direction is effortless thanks to the integrated Qibla compass, allowing you to locate Mecca from anywhere on the globe. Stay informed about significant Islamic holidays through automatic tracking and helpful countdowns. During the holy month, Ramadan Mode activates specialized features to enhance your fasting experience. Designed for a global audience, the app features full bilingual support in English and Arabic, including a native RTL layout for natural reading. All of this is wrapped in a beautiful, native iOS interface that fully supports dark mode for a truly modern premium feel.
